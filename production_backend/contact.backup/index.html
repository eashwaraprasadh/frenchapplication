Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about our courses and services.

How do I enroll in a course?

You can enroll directly through our website by selecting your desired course and completing the registration process. We accept all major payment methods.

What is your refund policy?

We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee for all courses. If you're not satisfied within the first 30 days, we'll provide a full refund.

Do you offer group discounts?

Yes! We offer special pricing for groups of 5 or more students. Contact us for a custom quote based on your group size and requirements.

Can I switch between course levels?

Absolutely. If you find your current level too easy or challenging, you can switch to a more appropriate level at any time during your enrollment.

Are the certificates internationally recognized?

Yes, our DELF/DALF and TCF preparation courses prepare you for internationally recognized certifications accepted worldwide.
